**Note:** Cesses and surcharges do not constitute part of the divisible pool of Central resources. An increase in cess and surcharges add to the pool of Union resources and are not passed to the states. Includes Education Cess, Swachh Bharat Cess, Krishi Kalyan Cess, Secondary and Higher Education Cess, Cess on Crude Oil, Bidi, Sugar, Automobiles, Clean Environmental Cess, Surcharge on Pan Masala and Tobacco Products etc.
